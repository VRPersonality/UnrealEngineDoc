14/**
15 * AIDataProvider is an object that can provide collection of properties
16 * associated with bound pawn owner or request Id.
17 *
18 * Editable properties are used to set up provider instance,
19 * creating additional filters or ways of accessing data (e.g. gameplay tag of ability)
20 *
21 * Non editable properties are holding data
22 */
