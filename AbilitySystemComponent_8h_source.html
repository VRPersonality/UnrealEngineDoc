37/**
38 * UAbilitySystemComponent
39 *
40 * A component to easily interface with the 3 aspects of the AbilitySystem:
41 *
42 * GameplayAbilities:
43 * -Provides a way to give/assign abilities that can be used (by a player or AI for example)
44 * -Provides management of instanced abilities (something must hold onto them)
45 * -Provides replication functionality
46 * -Ability state must always be replicated on the UGameplayAbility itself, but UAbilitySystemComponent provides RPC replication
47 * for the actual activation of abilities
48 *
49 * GameplayEffects:
50 * -Provides an FActiveGameplayEffectsContainer for holding active GameplayEffects
51 * -Provides methods for applying GameplayEffects to a target or to self
52 * -Provides wrappers for querying information in FActiveGameplayEffectsContainers (duration, magnitude, etc)
53 * -Provides methods for clearing/remove GameplayEffects
54 *
55 * GameplayAttributes
56 * -Provides methods for allocating and initializing attribute sets
57 * -Provides methods for getting AttributeSets
58 *
59 */
