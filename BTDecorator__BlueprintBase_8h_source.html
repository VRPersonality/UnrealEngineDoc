18/**
19 * Base class for blueprint based decorator nodes. Do NOT use it for creating native c++ classes!
20 *
21 * Unlike task and services, decorator have two execution chains:
22 * ExecutionStart-ExecutionFinish and ObserverActivated-ObserverDeactivated
23 * which makes automatic latent action cleanup impossible. Keep in mind, that
24 * you HAVE TO verify is given chain is still active after resuming from any
25 * latent action (like Delay, Timelines, etc).
26 *
27 * Helper functions:
28 * - IsDecoratorExecutionActive (true after ExecutionStart, until ExecutionFinish)
29 * - IsDecoratorObserverActive (true after ObserverActivated, until ObserverDeactivated)
30 */
31
