19/*
20 * Blend Two Keyframes Task
21 *
22 * This pops the top two keyframes from the VM keyframe stack, it blends/interpolates them, and pushes
23 * back the result onto the stack.
24 * Let B be the input keyframe at the top of the stack and A be the second from the top.
25 * Then we have:
26 * Result = Interpolate(A, B, Alpha)
27 * Top = Interpolate(Top-1, Top, Alpha)
28 * An Alpha of 0.0 returns A while 1.0 returns B
29 *
30 * Internally, this breaks down into the following tasks:
31 * * FAnimNextBlendOverwriteKeyframeWithScaleTask(1.0 - InterpolationAlpha) // todo clarify alpha
32 * * FAnimNextBlendAddKeyframeWithScaleTask(InterpolationAlpha)
33 * * FAnimNextNormalizeKeyframeRotationsTask
34 *
35 * In order to blend more than two keyframes together, simply add more FAnimNextBlendAddKeyframeWithScaleTask
36 * tasks each with its corresponding weight before normalizing rotations.
37 */
