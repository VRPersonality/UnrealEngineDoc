22/**
23 * Controllers are non-physical actors that can possess a Pawn to control
24 * its actions. PlayerControllers are used by human players to control pawns, while
25 * AIControllers implement the artificial intelligence for the pawns they control.
26 * Controllers take control of a pawn using their Possess() method, and relinquish
27 * control of the pawn by calling UnPossess().
28 *
29 * Controllers receive notifications for many of the events occurring for the Pawn they
30 * are controlling. This gives the controller the opportunity to implement the behavior
31 * in response to this event, intercepting the event and superseding the Pawn's default
32 * behavior.
33 *
34 * ControlRotation (accessed via GetControlRotation()), determines the viewing/aiming
35 * direction of the controlled Pawn and is affected by input such as from a mouse or gamepad.
36 *
37 * @see https://docs.unrealengine.com/latest/INT/Gameplay/Framework/Controller/
38 */
