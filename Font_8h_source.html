93/**
94 * A font object, for use by Slate, UMG, and Canvas.
95 *
96 * A font can either be:
97 * * Runtime cached - The font contains a series of TTF files that combine to form a composite font. The glyphs are cached on demand when required at runtime.
98 * * Offline cached - The font contains a series of textures containing pre-baked cached glyphs and their associated texture coordinates.
99 */
