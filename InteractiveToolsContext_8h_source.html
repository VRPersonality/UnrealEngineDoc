29/**
30 * InteractiveToolsContext owns the core parts of an Interactive Tools Framework implementation - the
31 * InputRouter, ToolManager, GizmoManager, TargetManager, and ContextStore. In the simplest
32 * use case, UInteractiveToolsContext is just a top-level container that will keep the various UObjects
33 * alive, and provide an easy way to access them. However in a more complex situation it may be
34 * desirable to subclass and extend the ToolsContext. For example, UEdModeInteractiveToolsContext allows
35 * a ToolsContext to live within a UEdMode.
36 */
37UCLASS(Transient, MinimalAPI)
