12 * @brief A structure that holds reflection information about a material.
13 * This structure is typically populated by the material translator as a side product of
14 * the translation process itself.
15 *
16 * You can use these insights for things like providing semantic colouring
17 * of the graph UI or accurately knowing what resources are referenced by the translated
