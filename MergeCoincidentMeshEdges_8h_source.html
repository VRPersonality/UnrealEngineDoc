19/**
20 * FMergeCoincidentMeshEdges finds pairs of boundary edges of the mesh that are identical
21 * (ie have endpoint vertices at the same locations) and merges the pair into a single edge.
22 * This is similar to welding vertices but safer because it prevents bowties from being formed.
23 *
24 * Currently if the two edges have the same "orientation" (ie from their respective triangles)
25 * they cannot be merged.
26 *
27 */
