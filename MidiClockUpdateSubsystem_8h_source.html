37/**
38 * Handles updating low-resolution play cursors associated with a MIDI clock. This now includes both FMidiClock
39 * and UMusicClockComponents.
40 * MidiClock: Because FMidiClock instances do not have their lifecycle managed by the garbage collector,
41 * we need a way to tick them on the game thread while avoiding races. This gives us a way to register clocks to be
42 * ticked from within their constructors/destructor, and provides no user-facing API.
43 * MusicClockComponent: Because the "current music time" is typically of interest to many game systems, and some of
44 * those systems run in parallel in different threads (e.g. TickComponent functions and animation jobs), it is
45 * important that the current music time is updated at the beginning of the game frame, and then that same time
46 * can be used for all systems for the frame.
47 * This subsystem solves for those problems.
48 */
