Parameters used when creating/updating the corresponding Material Parameter Collection.

Seconds from the beginning of the entire music authoring. Includes all count-in and pickup bars (ie. won't be negative when the music starts, and bar 1 beat 1 may not be at 0.0 seconds!

Bars from the beginning of the music. Includes all count-in and pickup bars (ie. won't be negative when the music starts, and bar 1 beat 1 of the music may not be equal to elapsed bar 0.0! NOTE: This Bar, unlike the Bar in the MusicTimestamp structure, is floating point and will include a fractional portion.

Total Beats from the beginning of the music. Includes all count-in and pickup bars/beats (ie. won't be negative when the music starts, and elapsed beat 0.0 may not equal a timestamp of bar 1 beat 1!

Seconds from Bar 1 Beat 1 of the music. If the music has a count-in or pickup bars this number may be negative when the music starts!

This is the bar member of the MusicTimestamp structure which represents a classic "music time" where bar 1 beat 1 is the beginning of the music AFTER count-in and pickups. If the music has a count-in or pickup bars this number may be negative (or zero) until bar 1 beat 1 is reached! NOTE: Unlike "BarsFromCountIn", Bar here will be an integer! This because it is the bar from the MusicTimestamp structure which also provides a "beat in bar" member to denote a "distance into the bar".

This is the beat member of the MusicTimestamp structure which represents a classic "music time" where bar 1 beat 1 is the beginning of the music AFTER count-in and pickups. If the music has a count-in or pickup bars this number may be negative (or zero) until bar 1 beat 1 is reached! NOTE: beat in this context is 1 based! The first beat in a bar is beat 1! Note: This is a floating point number.

Progress of the current bar [0, 1]. This is the same as the fractional part of BarsFromCountIn.

Progress of the current beat [0, 1]. This is the same as the fractional part of BeatsFromCountIn;

Current time signature numerator (beats per bar).

Current time signature denominator (scale from note duration to beat).
