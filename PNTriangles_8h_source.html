19 * FPNTriangles implements curved PN (Point-Normal) Triangles (Curved PN Triangles, Vlachos, 2001).
20 * Each PN triangle replaces one original flat triangle by a curved shape that is retriangulated into
21 * a number of small subtriangles. The geometry of a PN triangle is defined as one cubic Bezier patch using control
22 * points. The patch matches the point and normal information at the vertices of the original flat triangle.
23 */
