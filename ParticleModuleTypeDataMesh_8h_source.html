155 /**
156 * The camera facing option to use:
157 * All camera facing options without locked axis assume X-axis will be facing the camera.
158 * XAxisFacing_NoUp - X-axis camera facing, no attempt to face an axis up or down.
159 * XAxisFacing_ZUp - X-axis camera facing, Z-axis of the mesh should attempt to point up.
160 * XAxisFacing_NegativeZUp - X-axis camera facing, Z-axis of the mesh should attempt to point down.
161 * XAxisFacing_YUp - X-axis camera facing, Y-axis of the mesh should attempt to point up.
162 * XAxisFacing_NegativeYUp - X-axis camera facing, Y-axis of the mesh should attempt to point down.
163 * All axis-locked camera facing options assume the AxisLockOption is set. EPAL_NONE will be treated as EPAL_X.
164 * LockedAxis_ZAxisFacing - X-axis locked on AxisLockOption axis, rotate Z-axis of the mesh to face towards camera.
165 * LockedAxis_NegativeZAxisFacing - X-axis locked on AxisLockOption axis, rotate Z-axis of the mesh to face away from camera.
166 * LockedAxis_YAxisFacing - X-axis locked on AxisLockOption axis, rotate Y-axis of the mesh to face towards camera.
167 * LockedAxis_NegativeYAxisFacing - X-axis locked on AxisLockOption axis, rotate Y-axis of the mesh to face away from camera.
168 * All velocity-aligned options do NOT require the ScreenAlignment be set to PSA_Velocity.
169 * Doing so will result in additional work being performed... (it will orient the mesh twice).
170 * VelocityAligned_ZAxisFacing - X-axis aligned to the velocity, rotate the Z-axis of the mesh to face towards camera.
171 * VelocityAligned_NegativeZAxisFacing - X-axis aligned to the velocity, rotate the Z-axis of the mesh to face away from camera.
172 * VelocityAligned_YAxisFacing - X-axis aligned to the velocity, rotate the Y-axis of the mesh to face towards camera.
173 * VelocityAligned_NegativeYAxisFacing - X-axis aligned to the velocity, rotate the Y-axis of the mesh to face away from camera.
174 */
