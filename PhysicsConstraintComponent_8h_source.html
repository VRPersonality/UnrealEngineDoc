362 /**
363 * Set the projection settings for use when projection is enabled. See SetProjectionEnabled.
364 * For ragdolls you usually require that ProjectionLinearAlpha and ProjectionAngularAlpha be zero. They are most useful for cosmetic chains etc. ProjectionLinearTolerance and ProjectionAngularTolerance
365 * are emergency error recovery settings and should only rarely affect the simulation. If the simulation is significantly affected by the Tolerance settings, it likely indicates a setup stability issue.
366 *
367 * @param ProjectionLinearAlpha Controls the semi-physical correction of linear error remaining after the joint solve. Will add energy into the system. Best for joint chains connected to a kinematic.
368 * @param ProjectionAngularAlpha Controls the semi-physical correction of angular error remaining after the joint solve. Will add energy into the system. Best for joint chains connected to a kinematic.
369 * @param ProjectionLinearTolerance Linear errors above this will be corrected with a non-physical teleport.
370 * @param ProjectionAngularTolerance Angular errors above this will be corrected with a non-physical teleport.
