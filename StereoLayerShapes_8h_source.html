6/** Different layer shapes are defined as subclass of ILayerShape.
7 * Currently, only FQuadLayer is supported by all platforms, but plugins may define their own custom shapes.
8 * When encountering an unsupported shape type, implementations should treat the layer as a quad layer.
9 */
