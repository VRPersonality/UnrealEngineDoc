58 *
59 * When set, two pieces of generated text (eg, from FText::Format, FText::AsNumber, FText::AsDate, FText::ToUpper, etc)
60 * will test their internal data to see if they contain identical inputs (so would produce an identical output).

62 * When clear, no two separate pieces of generated text will ever compare as identical!
