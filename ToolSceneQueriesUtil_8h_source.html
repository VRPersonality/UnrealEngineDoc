40 /**
41 * Get a measurement for testing whether two points can snap together, useful for choosing the best snap point among multiple
42 * options. For perspective mode, the returned metric is the "visual angle" between the points, which is the angle between the points
43 * to the camera scaled such that the visual angle between the horizontal bounds of the view is 90. For orthographic mode, it is a
44 * projected distance onto the view plane scaled such that the distance between the horizontal bounds of the view is 90.
45 * Thus, the metric is suitable for comparing against a visual angle snap threshold to determine if snapping should happen.
46 */
47 MODELINGCOMPONENTS_API double PointSnapMetric(const FViewCameraState& CameraState, const FVector3d& Point1, const FVector3d& Point2);
