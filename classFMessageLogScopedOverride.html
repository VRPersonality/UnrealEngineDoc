Detailed Description

Scoped override for FMessageLog behavior.

This can override the log behavior of a given named FMessageLog for the duration of the overrides lifetime, optionally suppressing output log mirroring, or promoting/demoting log categories (eg, to make errors act as warnings, or warnings act as errors).
