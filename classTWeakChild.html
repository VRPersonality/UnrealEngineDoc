Detailed Description

template<typename ChildType>
class TWeakChild< ChildType >

For widgets that do not own their content, but are responsible for presenting someone else's content.

e.g. Tooltips are just presented by the owner window; not actually owned by it. They can go away at any time and then they'll just stop being shown.
