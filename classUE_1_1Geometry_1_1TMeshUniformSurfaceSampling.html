Sample a random triangle, with probability proportional to relative triangle area.

Note: Use VectorUtil::UniformSampleTrianglePoint to generate a uniform random point on a given triangle, or VectorUtil::UniformSampleTriangleBarycentricCoords for a uniform random barycentric coordinate

Parameters UniformRandom1, UniformRandom2 must be in the [0,1) range.
