Detailed Description

template<typename NumericType>
class SSpinBox< NumericType >

A Slate SpinBox resembles traditional spin boxes in that it is a widget that provides keyboard-based and mouse-based manipulation of a numeric value.

Mouse-based manipulation: drag anywhere on the spinbox to change the value. Keyboard-based manipulation: click on the spinbox to enter text mode.
