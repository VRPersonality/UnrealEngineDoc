79 /** At above what density level a tile is allowed to force collapse even if not all leaf nodes in the subtree are present.
80 * Collapsing will not occus if any child node in the subtree has different materials.
81 * Setting this to -1 means no collapsing is allowed and the water mesh will always keep it's silhouette at any distance.
82 * Setting this to 0 will allow every level to collapse
83 * Setting this to something higher than the LODCount will have no effect
84 */
