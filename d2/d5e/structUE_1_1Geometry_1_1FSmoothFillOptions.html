◆ InteriorSmoothness

double UE::Geometry::FSmoothFillOptions::InteriorSmoothness = 0.2

Controls the trade off between smoothness in the fill region vs faithfulness to the original fill surface.

This value is inversely proportional to the vertex constraint weight in Laplacian smoothing, for vertices in the fill interior far away from the boundary.
