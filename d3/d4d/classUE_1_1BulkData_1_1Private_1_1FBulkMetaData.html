Detailed Description

Bulk meta data, size, offset, flags and lock status packed in 16 bytes.

Uses 5 bytes for size and offset. [0 - 4][5 - 9][ 10 ][ 11 ][12 - 15]([16 - 23]) [Size][Offset][Unused][LockFlags][BulkDataFlags]([SizeOnDisk])
