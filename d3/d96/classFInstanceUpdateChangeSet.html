FInstanceDataFlags FInstanceUpdateChangeSet::Flags

Flags that dictate what attributes will be gathered from the source (and deposited at the destination).

These must be present during gather (or they should have been disabled earlier). Constructed as the intersection of the flags indicated by the component & what is supported by the proxy(?).

FInstanceDataFlags FInstanceUpdateChangeSet::ForceFullFlags

Flags that can be set to force individual attributes to use a full update has effect IFF Flags is set for the given attribute.

If an attribute was enabled after not being used (e.g., on a material change, perhaps) a full update must be sent.
