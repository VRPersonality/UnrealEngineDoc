320/**
321 * UCombinedTransformGizmo provides standard Transformation Gizmo interactions,
322 * applied to a UTransformProxy target object. By default the Gizmo will be
323 * a standard XYZ translate/rotate Gizmo (axis and plane translation).

325 * The in-scene representation of the Gizmo is a ACombinedTransformGizmoActor (or subclass).
