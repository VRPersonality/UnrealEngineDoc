23/**
24 * The base class for custom spawnable bindings. A spawnable binding will spawn an object upon resolution or return a cached previously spawned object.
25 * UMovieSceneSpawnableActorBinding is the reimplementation of previous FMovieSceneSpawnable features and spawns an actor based on a saved template and actor class.
26 * Otherwise, projects are free to implement their own Spawnable bindings by overriding this class.
27 * In doing so, they could choose to just override GetSpawnObjectClass, PostSpawnObject, and PreDestroyObject for example to do custom post-spawn setup on a character mesh,
28 * or they could choose to fully override SpawnObject and DestroySpawnedObject and do their own custom logic for spawning completely.
29 */
