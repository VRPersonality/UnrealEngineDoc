ConcatenateRules< TransformTypeA, TransformTypeB >::ResultType Concatenate ( const TransformTypeA & LHS,
const TransformTypeB & RHS )
inlinenodiscard

Concatenates two transforms.

Uses TransformCast<> to convert them first. If more efficient means are available to concatenate two transforms, provide a non-template overload (or possibly a specialization). Concatenation is performed in left to right order, so the output space of LHS must match the input space of RHS.

Parameters
LHSTransformation that goes from space A to space B
RHSTransformation that goes from space B to space C.
Returns
a new transform representing the transformation from the input space of LHS to the output space of RHS.
