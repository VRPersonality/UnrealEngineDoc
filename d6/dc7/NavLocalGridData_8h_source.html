11/**
12 * Local navigation grid - simple 2D grid used for navigation.
13 *
14 * Cell can be either free or marked as obstacle, connected with 8 neighbors (no walls in between)
15 *
16 * When used as source for UNavLocalGridManager, each obstacle should define its own grid data.
17 * Corresponding category in gameplay debugger is hidden by default, please adjust project configs to change that.
