Detailed Description

FSubRegionRemesher is an extension of FRemesher that allows for constraining remeshing to a localized region of a mesh.

Currently this is initialized from a Vertex ROI.

Warning
Currently "boundary" edges of the ROI that are split will result in the ROI growing to include both new edges created by the split.
