47 /**
48 * Whether or not to render directly to the window's backbuffer or an offscreen render target that is applied to the window later
49 * Rendering to an offscreen target is the most common option in the editor where there may be many frames which this viewport's interface may wish to not re-render but use a cached buffer instead
50 * Rendering directly to the backbuffer is the most common option in the game where you want to update each frame without the cost of writing to an intermediate target first.
