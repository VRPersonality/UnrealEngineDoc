14 /** FOperatorSettings
15 *
16 * Audio settings for Metasound IOperators including audio sample rate,
17 * block rate and frames per block.
18 *
19 * The audio SampleRate defines the number of audio samples per a second of
20 * a mono waveform.
21 *
22 * The BlockRate defines the number of times an IOperator's execution
23 * function will be triggered per a second of audio content.
24 *
25 * The NumFramesPerBlock defines the number of audio frames written-to
26 * and/or read-from during a single call to an IOperator's execution
27 * function.
28 *
29 * GetNumFramesPerBlock() is required to return a value that abides by the
30 * alignment requirements of FAudioBuffer, FAlignedFloatBuffer and the math
31 * routines declared in BufferVectorOperations.h. This enables the vast
32 * majority of Metasound IOperators to take advantage of SIMD hardware
33 * accelerations.
34 *
35 * In order to achieve SIMD alignment, desired block rates must be slightly
36 * adjusted to match the boundaries of the given sample rate and SIMD
37 * alignment. GetActualBlockRate() returns the true block rate after
38 * adhering to sample rate and SIMD boundaries.
39 */
