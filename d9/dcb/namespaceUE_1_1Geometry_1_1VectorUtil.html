Compute barycentric coordinates/weights of vPoint inside 2D triangle (V0,V1,V2).

If point is inside triangle, coords will be positive and sum to 1. ie if result is a, then vPoint = a.x*V0 + a.y*V1 + a.z*V2. TODO: make robust to degenerate triangles?
