Detailed Description

Helper struct to sub step through Montages when advancing time.

These require stopping at sections and branching points to potential jumps and loops. And also stepping through TimeStretchMarkers to adjust play rate based on TimeStretchCurve.

float FMontageSubStepper::FindMontagePosition_Original ( float In_P_Target)
private

Finds montage position in 'original' space, given current position in 'target' space.

This means given a montage playback time, we find its actual position. This should only be used for montage position, as we cache results and lazily update it for performance.
