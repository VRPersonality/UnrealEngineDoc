13/**
14 * This component lets us store replicated information about how any particular UPrimitiveComponent
15 * should be attached to its parent cluster union. The benefits of using a separate components are:
16 *
17 * 1) It lets us avoid adding any additional overhead into the UPrimitiveComponent.
18 * 2) It lets the replicated information have the same net relevancy as the actor being added to the cluster union
19 * rather than having the same net relevancy as the cluster union (i.e. in the case of replicating this data in
20 * an array in the ClusterUnionComponent).
21 * 3) It lets us pinpoint what exactly is being added/removed (vs if all this data was stored in an array) which lets
22 * us be a bit more efficient in terms of modifying the cluster union.
23 */
