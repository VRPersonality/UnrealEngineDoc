18/**
19 * Global (one per-UWorld) manager object that manages ticking and updating any and all Sequencer-based
20 * evaluations for the current frame, before any other actors are ticked.
21 *
22 * Ticking clients are registered based on their desired tick interval, and grouped together with other
23 * clients that tick with the same interval (based on Sequencer.TickIntervalGroupingResolutionMs).
24 *
25 * Sequencer data is shared between all instances within the same group, allowing them to blend together.
26 * Clients ticking at different intervals do not support blending with each other.
27 */
