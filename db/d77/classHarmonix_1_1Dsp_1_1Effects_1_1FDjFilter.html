Parameters::TParameter<float> Harmonix::Dsp::Effects::FDjFilter::Amount { -1.0f, 1.0f, 0.0f }

The knob position.

-1 to the dead zone will low-pass the signal. Dead zone to 1 will high-pass the signal. Within the dead zone will fade to dry.
