Detailed Description

Represents an abstract triangle patch with corners [u,v,w], s.t.

u = (1,0,0), v = (0,1,0), w = (0,0,1). Contains information about the barycentric coordinates and IDs of the new vertices and the new triangles generated during the tessellation.
