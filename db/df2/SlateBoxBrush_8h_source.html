9/**
10 * A 3x3 box where the sides stretch horizontally and vertically and the middle stretches to fill.
11 * The corners will not be stretched. The size of the sides and corners is determined by the
12 * Margin as follows:
13 *
14 * _____________________
15 * | | Margin.Top | |
16 * |__|_______________|__| Margin.Right
17 * | | | | /
18 * +--> | | <--+
19 * / |__|_______________|__|

22 *
23 */
