139 /**
140 * UpdateAfterGeometryEdit should be called after editing the UDynamicMesh owned by the Selector (TargetMesh).
141 * This may be an external DynamicMesh in the case of a DynamicMeshComponent, or a temporary UDynamicMesh in
142 * the case of (eg) the StaticMeshSelector and VolumeSelector subclasses. In DynamicMeshSelector the MeshChange
143 * can just be emitted as a transaction, this is the default behavior. However in StaticMeshSelector, the StaticMesh
144 * needs to be synchronized with the UDynamicMesh modification in the same transaction. And potentially the MeshChange
145 * does not need to be emitted at all in that case.
146 *
147 * This is a bit ugly and might be possible to do more cleanly by having the StaticMeshSelector listen to the
148 * UDynamicMesh for changes. However currently we do not have the granularity to have it /only/ listen for
149 * external mesh edit changes, and not all changes (and since the UDynamicMesh changes in response to
150 * StaticMesh changes, eg on undo or external edits, it creates a cycle).
151 */
