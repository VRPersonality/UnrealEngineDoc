11/**
12 * This component tries to maintain its children at a fixed distance from the parent,
13 * but will retract the children if there is a collision, and spring back when there is no collision.
14 *
15 * Example: Use as a 'camera boom' or 'selfie stick' to keep the follow camera for a player from colliding into the world.
16 */
17
