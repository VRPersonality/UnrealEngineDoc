Detailed Description

Specifies an entry to a specific piece of pre-animated state within a FPreAnimatedStateExtension instance.

An entry is represented by a group handle (usually relating to a UObject* retrieved from FPreAnimatedObjectGroupManager) and the handle to the specific storage entry
