Detailed Description

FSceneSamples stores samples corresponding to pixels in the DeviceDepth photoset where the values are strictly within the viewing frustum, which corresponds to a camera ray intersecting an actor in VisibleActors.

All non-null containers with Computed capture status will be filled by GetSceneSamples() and will have the same .Num() counts
