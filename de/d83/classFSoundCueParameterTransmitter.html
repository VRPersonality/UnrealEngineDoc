TArray< FAudioParameter > FSoundCueParameterTransmitter::ReleaseAccumulatedParameterUpdates ( )

The SoundCueParameter keeps a running track of parameter changes from SetParameters(...).

When this method is called, the accumulated parameters are returned and the internal tracking of accumulated parameters is reset.
