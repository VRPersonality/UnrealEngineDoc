TAttribute<bool> FMenuEntryResizeParams::AllowClipping

If true (default), allow this entry to be clipped during resizing of toolbars.

If false, this entry will never be clipped and always stay in the toolbar.

◆ ClippingPriority

TAttribute<int32> FMenuEntryResizeParams::ClippingPriority

The priority of this entry during resizing (default is 0).

A higher priority relative to other entries keeps the entry visible for longer as size is constrained during toolbar resizing.
