◆ SampleQueue

TSharedPtr<FMediaTextureSampleQueue> FMovieSceneMediaData::SampleQueue
private

Sample queue to be used as video sink for the media player.

It is owned by the media section because it must be set in the player prior to entering the playback section, i.e. in preroll. We can't ue the internal media texture sample queue because it is in use by another section during this section's preroll. Each section needs it's sample queue independently of the media texture.
