Detailed Description

template<typename ScalarType>
struct RenderingSpatialHash::TLocation< ScalarType >

Describes an object location in the rendering hierarchical spatial hash grid.

The location cosists of an integer 3D coordinate and a Level which is derived such that the size of the bounds are at most 1 (integer) unit at that level. Put differently, the Level is calculated as the FloorLog2(Size).
